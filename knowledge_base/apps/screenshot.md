# Screenshot Tool

The Screenshot tool captures images of your screen for sharing or saving instructions.

Common tasks:
- Open the screenshot tool: search for **Screenshot** in the app list.
- Capture the whole screen: choose **Full Screen** and click **Take Screenshot**.
- Capture a window: choose **Window** and select the window to capture.
- Capture a selection: choose **Selection** and drag to select an area.
- Save the image: choose a folder (usually **Pictures**) and save.

Tips:
- If you need to show a step, take a screenshot and attach it to your message.
