# Settings (System Settings)

The Settings app lets you adjust system preferences like Wi-Fi, display, and sound.

Common tasks:
- Connect to Wi-Fi: **Settings > Wi-Fi** and select a network.
- Change language: **Settings > Language & Region**.
- Adjust brightness: **Settings > Display** and move the brightness slider.
- Change sound volume: **Settings > Sound**.
- Check storage: **Settings > Storage**.

Tips:
- If an app is too small, increase **Display Scale** in **Display** settings.
